About Astrophiles

A place for astronomy lovers, math enthusiasts, and curious minds to connect.

Our Mission

Astrophiles was created to bring together people passionate about space, mathematics, and physics. We aim to share knowledge, encourage collaboration, and inspire curiosity about the universe.

What We Do

  • Host discussions about astronomy, astrophysics, and related sciences.
  • Share learning resources, tutorials, and libraries.
  • Run events and projects like our Minecraft server to encourage creativity.
